Child Care Monitoring

One-stop solution for children and pregnant women’s health monitoring

Women going through their pregnancy is crucial and require continuous health monitoring and immediate medical attention. It's said that prevention is better than cure so after a mother has given birth to a child, they cannot express themselves unless they learn to talk, so constantly monitoring will help in detecting medical issues at an early age.

Specific age leads to specific development in children; the child health monitoring feature provides parents with a one-stop medical solution for tracking their kid’s immunization shots, growth, and development.

It also helps in curating a systematic plan for medicines and vaccinations. It will also provide to-be mothers to track their pregnancy, provide timely medication reminders, and monitor their health before and after pregnancy accurately for holistic care. Our utmost priority is mother and kid’s medical care with safety and accuracy.

Medical Record Tracking

Get your hands on Medical Records

Much difficulty starts from revisiting the old files full of manual prescriptions and efficiently maintaining a track of your medical history but don't worry, we at Zelon have a feature for it, an integrated healthcare ecosystem.

This technology will let you upload past medical records, compile them and store them safely in one place under the same Unique Identification Number (UIN), making them accessible anytime and anywhere. We provide you with trusted automated or manual medical recording tracking where at one click, you can share the data with doctors near you or with any other medical health providers.

These old records are not just pieces of paper but provide valuable insights into your future health examination. You are in safe hands as this is a fully proven technologically driven software that will help you keep track of your medical history systematically. This platform will bring patients, expert doctors, and other health professionals together. Click on the feature and explore more.
